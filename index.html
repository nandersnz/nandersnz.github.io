the art of architecture

I’m an architect. I like saying that. It conjures visions of grand buildings, gravity defying sky-scrappers, and intricate bridges. Then I remind myself that I’m not that type of architect, that the systems that I work with are (mostly) virtual, no less spectacular, but lacking a physical element that can make it hard to describe the vision. That’s where the art of enterprise architecture comes in.
